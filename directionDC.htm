How to get the the Rehersal Dinner from the Church:

1) Exit the church parking lot and turn right tn LakeShore Blvd.

2) Make an almost immediate Left on to East 200 St.

3) Travel about 1 mile untill you encounter I-90.

4) Go under the bridge and turn left onto the marginal road.

5) Take the marginal road onto I-90 East.

6) Take State Route 2 East at the I-90 split in Euclid. You will just go strait as I-90 viers to the right.

7) Exit at Route 91, SOM Center Road, Willoughby / Eastlake.

8) Turn Right at the light. Go about .5 mile to Euclid Ave. There will be a Denny's and a BP at the corner.

9) At the stop light, turn left on to Euclid Ave, Route 20.

10) Take Euclid Ave about 2 miles into downtown Willoughby. You will Pass a Lowe's on the right, and a big plaza on the left. Once you see a little park on the right, and white tree lights along the road, you are in downtown.

11) Turn right on to Glenn Ave / 2nd St. It will be the second light in the downtown area.

12) After the storefront on the left ends, you will see the Willoughby Brewing Company back to the left. You can park in the lot in front of the Brewing company. There is an overflow lot across Glenn Street.
