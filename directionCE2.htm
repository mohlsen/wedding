How to get to the Church from the East / South East:

If you are coming from the East or South East (such as Pittsburgh or Philly):

1) Come toward Ohio via I-80 West or I-76 West (76 is the PA Turn Pike.)

2) If you're on 76 W, take 76 to the Ohio Turn Pike (I-80). If you're on 80 W, take 80 past Youngstown. Stay on I-80 and it will turn into the Turn Pike.

3) Take the Turn Pike (I-80 at this point) about 35 miles to I-480 West.

4) Exit the Turn Pike and travel on I-480 W 10 miles until it turns into I-271 North.

5) Once on I-271 North, travel about 20 miles.

NOTE: OPTIONAL - I-271 has express lanes. You can take the express lanes, but be careful, as it is very easy to miss the I-90 entrance back onto the standard lanes.

6) Exit onto I-90 West.

7) Continue on I-90 West for 3 miles toward Cleveland.

8) Exit at exit # 182B, East 200th St in Euclid.

9) The exit will put you on a marginal road that parallels I-90. Take this marginal road to East 200th St and Turn right (north) onto East 200th. There will be a big Honda dealer and Home Depot on the right.

10) Take East 200th for about 1 mile until it dead ends at a stop light into LakeShore Blvd. You will see the church directly in front of you.

11) The parking for the church is to the right of the church as you are looking at it. Turn right onto LakeShore Blvd and make an almost an immediate left on to East 201st St. to take you to the parking entrance.
