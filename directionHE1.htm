How to get to the Hotels from the East / North East:

If you are coming from the East or North East (such as Meadeville or New York):

1) Take I-90 West Toward Cleveland.

2) Take the OH-91 exit- exit number 189- toward WILLOUGHBY HILLS/ WILLOUGHBY

3) The exit ramp will come to a stop light, turn right to head north on OH-91 / SOM Center Rd.

4) At the first light, turn right on to MapleGrove Rd. This will be immediately after the Eat 'N Park and Cracker Barrel.

5) You will see the two hotels. The Fairfield is on the right, the Courtyard is on the left.
