The Ohlsen Family

The Ohlsen’s family lives in Wooster, about an hour south of Cleveland. They have resided there for about 15 years after moving from Meadeville PA. It’s a cute little town that has given Mike and now Becky an appreciation for the country.
  Diane Ohlsen

Mike’s mother, Diane, is a First Grade teacher for Wooster City Schools. Like Becky’s mom, she has been a teacher her whole life, but stayed home with the children until they were in school full time. She has now been teaching those little ones down in Wayne County for about 15 years. Many people would say that a first grade teacher does not have much impact on such young children, but after watching Diane interact with her current and past students, it is obvious that her early childhood teaching skills have influenced many lives. She is so involved in her work, that Diane actually goes to bed anxiously waiting for the next day to begin! Almost all of her spare time activities involve working on her classroom and preparing for the next lesson. When she does get away from the classroom, Diane enjoys reading, going on walks, and sleeping in the hammock.

Mike Ohlsen

Big Mike, as they like to call him, is a native of western Pennsylvania and southern New Jersey. Once you hear him ask for a glass or “watta” (water) or suggest you use a “coopon” (coupon) at a grocery store, you will certainly hear the accent. He is now an industrial engineer at the Will-Burt Company located in Orrville, OH. Mike is a hands-on guy who loves to tinker around with tools and cars. He is an all around handy man, who would be glad to help any stranger off the block with their household chores. In his spare time, Mike likes to watch Philadelphia and Cleveland based sporting events, and complains that his team never wins. After the games are over you will usually find Mike asleep on the couch with an old Star Trek re-run on the television.
Suzi Ohlsen

Suzi is Mike’s younger sister by almost five years. Growing up, the age difference kept the two at odds most of the time, bickering and teasing one another. Maybe Mike used to pick on her because she tried to cut his ear off with a pair of plastic scissors at the age of three (ask any Ohlsen for the full story!) But as they have grown older, Mike and Suzi have grown much closer to one another due, in part, to Becky. Suzi is currently a junior at Wittenberg University majoring in Early Childhood Education. She hopes to someday marry a rich man so she does not have to work and support her spending habits.
