Becky and Mike

 

   Here she is, the bride to be, Becky

Rebecca Theresa Hren was born at Euclid General Hospital on April 12, 1979 in Euclid, Ohio. Becky attended the Euclid City Schools from kindergarten until she graduated in 1997. While there she was involved in multitudes of activities including cheerleading and swim team. After graduation Becky attended Ohio Northern University in Ada, Ohio. She graduated in 2003 with a PharmD (Doctor of Pharmacy) degree. Becky accepted a clinical pharmacist position with Lake Hospital System and has been there since the beginning of June. Her office is located at Lake East in Painesville and that is where she does a majority of her work with the physicians, nurses and other pharmacists. In her spare time she loves to shop, shop, shop, clean like a crazy lady, ski, watch replay TV, and go out to eat with her Mike!

Here’s Mike…

Michael Ohlsen (better known as Mick to his family, Mojo or Mohlsen to his buds and as Jimmy to the Hren family [do not ask]) has an obvious identity crisis going on. Mike was born on June 29, 1977 in Meadville, Pennsylvania. He lived in Meadville for 10 years before his family packed up and headed to Wooster, Ohio. There Mike finished his schooling and graduated in 1996 from Wooster High School. From there Mike was off to Ohio Northern University to attain his Electrical/Computer Science Engineering degree. Mike currently works for an engineering firm called Fenetech in Aurora, Ohio. It is there that he utilizes all his nerdy computer skills to crank out new software. In Mike’s spare time he enjoys watching and playing hockey, playing on the numerous computers he has at home along with networking the house, getting the most unbelievable deals online, and going out with Becky.

  

  It was during Becky's freshman year and Mike's sophomore year that they met. It was early March of 1998, the first weekend of Spring quarter that they met at a sorority/fraternity party. Beck is a Delta Zeta and Mike is a member of Delta Sigma Phi. Mike and Becky agree that they met in the above way, but do not ask the two when their first kiss was because you will get two completely different answers!
