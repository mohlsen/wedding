How to get to the Church from the South:

1) Come to downtown Cleveland via I-71 North or I-77 North

2) I-71 will turn into I-90 East. I-77 will come to a T with I-90, exit onto I-90 East.

3) Continue out of Cleveland for 10 Miles on I-90 East

4) Exit at exit # 182B, East 200th St in Euclid.

5) Take the marginal road to East 200th St and Turn left (north) onto East 200th at the stop light. You will travel under a bridge and see a big Honda Dealer and Home Depot on the right.

6) Take East 200th for about 1 mile until it dead ends at a stop light into LakeShore Blvd. You will see the church directly in front of you.

7) The parking for the church is to the right of the church as you are looking at it. Turn right onto LakeShore Blvd and make an almost an immediate left on to East 201st St. to take you to the parking entrance.
