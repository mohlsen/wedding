How to get the the Reception from the East / North East:

If you are coming from the East or North East (such as Meadeville or New York):

1) Take I-90 West Toward Cleveland.

2) Take the OH-91 exit- exit number 189- toward WILLOUGHBY HILLS/ WILLOUGHBY

3) The exit ramp will come to a stop light, turn left to head south on OH-91 / SOM Center Rd.

4) Go South about 1 mile to Chardon Rd

5) Turn right at the stop light for Chardon Rd.

6) Go about .5 mile. Shortly after you go on a bridge over I-271, La-Vera Part Center will be on the left.

7) Enter on the right hand side of the building, the parking lot is in the back.
