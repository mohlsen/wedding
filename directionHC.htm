How to get to the Hotels from the Church:

1) Exit the church parking lot and turn right on LakeShore Blvd.

2) Make an almost immediate Left on to East 200 St.

3) Travel about 1 mile untill you encounter I-90.

4) Go under the bridge and turn left onto the marginal road.

5) Take the marginal road onto I-90 East.

6) Stay on I-90 at the SR 2 split in Euclid. You will just veir to the right.

7) Continue on I-90 East for an additional 4 miles. Stay on I-90 at the I-271 split, you will just veir to the left.

8) Take the OH-91 exit- exit number 189- toward WILLOUGHBY HILLS/ WILLOUGHBY

9) The exit ramp will come to a stop light, turn right to head north on OH-91 / SOM Center Rd.

10) At the second light, turn right on to MapleGrove Rd. This will be immediately after the Eat 'N Park and Cracker Barrel.

11) You will see the two hotels. The Fairfield is on the right, the Courtyard is on the left.
