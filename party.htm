Seth Voit

Mike met Seth on the very first night of college at Ohio Northern University when Seth came stumbling into his room after a night on the town. Let's just say things have not changed much over the years! As the years went on at Ohio Northern Mike and Seth became close friends as they were in the fraternity (Delta Sigma Phi) together and they had the exact same major / minor. They were nearly in every class together, were lab partners in nearly every lab, and shared an office their final year. Without each other, many people think that neither Mike or Seth would have graduated from school. Seth currently works for American Electric Power in Columbus. However, he will be leaving AEP this fall to attend the University of Dayton Law School. Mike is going to be waiting for Seth to give him a late night call when he needs a study break. At the end of June, Seth was married to a great Philly girl, Heather!

Jarrod Holtzapple

Jarrod is another college friend that goes back a long ways. He is another fraternity brother, that has done a lot to support Mike over the years. Sarah Engelman

Sarah has been one of Becky's closest friends for the last five years. Becky and Sarah met at Ohio Northern University when they both rushed and pledged the same sorority, Delta Zeta. Their friendship did not blossom right away; it was not until their sophomore that they found out they had so very much in common, including their major, pharmacy. After that they were roommates for the next three years of pharmacy school and had a blast in every place they lived. Sarah graduated from pharmacy school this past year with Becky, making her a doctor too! She currently works for Kroger pharmacy in Columbus Ohio as a floating pharmacist. In Sarah's spare time she enjoys shopping, running and spending time with her boyfriend of many years, Jeff!

  

Kent Lee

Kent has been a childhood friend of Mike's since they met back in junior high. All through high school, Kent and Mike worked together on all kinds of nerdy stuff. In fact, many of Mike's technology skills were taught to him by Kent (including web pages!) All though college, Mike and Kent kept in contact, sharing their new found engineering knowledge quite frequently. Kent graduated from Case Western Reserve University in 2001 with a Bachelors degree in Biomedical Engineering and a Master's degree in Electrical engineering. He now lives in Minneapolis, Minnesota and works for the Guidant Corporation bringing you the latest in heart monitoring and sleep apnea technologies. In his spare time, Kent enjoys traveling the world with a discount budget to places like England, Taiwan, Mainland China, and Singapore. Someday, Becky and Mike hope to travel with Kent around the world.

  
Brooke Rasmussen

Brooke, like Sarah, has been one of Becky's closest friends for the last five years. They too met at Ohio Northern University when they both rushed and pledged the same sorority, Delta Zeta. Their friendship really began to flourish during their sophomore year when they lived across the hall from each other on "the floor." The floor was just a floor in the dorms on which all the sophomore Delta Zetas lived. After that they lived together in the DZ house and finally during Brooke's senior year (Becky's P4 or fourth year) in the house at 330 W. Buckeye. That year at 330 was AWESOME and is truly where some of the best college memories were made daily! Brooke graduated in 2001 from the Ohio Northern College of Business and less than one year later got her nursing home administrator license. Brooke currently works as the head administrator for a nursing home in Findlay, Ohio. In Brooke's spare time she enjoys working out, cleaning and spending time on the farm with her man, Travis.
