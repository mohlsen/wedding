How to get the the Reception from the West:

1) Take I-90 East to downtown Cleveland.

2) Continue on I-90 East toward the far east side for about 13 miles

3) Stay on I-90 at the SR 2 split in Euclid. You will just veir to the right.

4) Continue on I-90 East for an additional 4 miles. Stay on I-90 at the I-271 split, you will just veir to the left.

5) Take the OH-91 exit- exit number 189- toward WILLOUGHBY HILLS/ WILLOUGHBY

6) The exit ramp will come to a stop light, turn left to head south on OH-91 / SOM Center Rd.

7) Go South about 1 mile to Chardon Rd

8) Turn right at the stop light for Chardon Rd.

9) Go about .5 mile. Shortly after you go on a bridge over I-271, La-Vera Part Center will be on the left.

10) Enter on the right hand side of the building, the parking lot is in the back.
