Willoughby Brewing Company

4057 Erie St., Willoughby, OH 44094
 

Lake County's First Brewery Since Prohibition

Nestled in the heart of Historic Downtown Willoughby, Ohio lies The Willoughby Brewing Co. The Brewing Company was established in February of 1998, and is a full-service restaurant, bar, and award-winning brewery. It is located in a 100-year-old building that was originally a rail-car repair depot for the Cleveland to Ashtabula Interurban Rail Line.

The Brewing Company has been a favorite of Becky and Mike’s since its opening in 1998. They went on one of their first dates there and continue to enjoy it today. It was not until last year that the Ohlsen family was introduced to the Brewing Company and fell MADLY in love with it. They will drive over an hour and fifteen minutes just to have their perogies and hot pretzels. It is for all these reasons that the Brewing Company was the perfect choice for the rehearsal dinner!
