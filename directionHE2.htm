How to get to the Hotels from the East / South East:

If you are coming from the East or South East (such as Pittsburgh or Philly):

1) Come toward Ohio via I-80 West or I-76 West (76 is the PA Turn Pike.)

2) If you're on 76 W, take 76 to the Ohio Turn Pike (I-80). If you're on 80 W, take 80 past Youngstown. Stay on I-80 and it will turn into the Turn Pike.

3) Take the Turn Pike about 35 miles to I-480 West.

4) Exit the Turn Pike and travel on I-480 W 10 miles until it turns into I-271 North.

5) Once on I-271 North, travel about 20 miles.

NOTE: I-271 has express lanes with limited exits. Be careful as the exit we need for the hotels is not an exit from the express lanes.

6) I-271N will turn into I-90 East.

7) Take the OH-91 exit- exit number 189- toward WILLOUGHBY HILLS/ WILLOUGHBY

8) The exit ramp will come to a stop light, turn right to head north on OH-91 / SOM Center Rd.

9) At the second light, turn right on to MapleGrove Rd. This will be immediately after the Eat 'N Park and Cracker Barrel.

10) You will see the two hotels. The Fairfield is on the right, the Courtyard is on the left.
