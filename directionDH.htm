How to get the the Rehersal Dinner from the Hotels:

1) Exit the hotels and take MapleGrove Rd toward SOM Center Rd / OH-91.

2) Turn Right onto SOM Center Rd / OH-91.

3) Go about 2 miles to Euclid Ave, Route 20. There will be a Denny's and a BP at the corner.

4) At the stop light, turn right on to Euclid Ave, Route 20.

5) Take Euclid Ave about 2 miles into downtown Willoughby. You will Pass a Lowe's on the right, and a big plaza on the left. Once you see a little park on the right, and white tree lights along the road, you are in downtown.

6) Turn right on to Glenn Ave / 2nd St. It will be the second light in the downtown area.

7) After the storefront on the left ends, you will see the Willoughby Brewing Company back to the left. You can park in the lot in front of the Brewing company. There is an overflow lot across Glenn Street.
