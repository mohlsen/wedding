The Hren Family
 

 
Well these are Becky’s parents. Some say that she looks nothing like them (mostly because of her blonde hair) but when she is around them it is obvious that they are her folks!
Rudy Hren Rudy is an Investigator for the Department of Labor in Cleveland, Ohio. He has worked there ever since Becky has been alive which means he is almost done working forever. He is excited to be retiring soon and plans on watching more reruns of This Old House along with building their new house in Chardon. Presently, in his free time now he loves to watch PBS, drink beer, go bowling, and harass Becky!
   
Terri Hren Terri is a Special Education teacher and department chair at Garfield Heights High School in Garfield Heights., Ohio. She has been a teacher Becky’s whole life but did not go back to work full time until Becky was in high school. (Authors Note: I believe that is why Tony and I are both so successful). Although she was not teaching for money for all those years she was busy being a teacher at home to both Becky and her big brother, Tony. In Terri’s free time she enjoys shopping (always getting the best deals in town), crafting and watching other people’s cable television!
Rudy and Terri have been happily married for over 25 years and have been the best parents Becky could ever ask for!
 
This is Becky’s only brother, Tony, his wife of three years, Kelly, and their new baby, Anthony Junior (A.J.). They currently live in St. Clair, Minnesota, which is about two hours south of the Twin Cities. With them in Minnesota they have a fish and two dogs.
Tony Hren Tony is a mechanical engineer and product owner for GE. He graduated from Case Western Reserve University about five years ago and has been with GE ever since. However you would not know that he has been with the same company for all this time because he and his family have moved three times and are getting ready to move again. Tony and Becky were best buds growing up and continue to have a very close relationship. It is for this reason that Tony is Becky’s best person for the wedding. They were as close as any sisters could be so why not have Tony stand by Becky’s side.
   
Kelly Hren Presently Kelly has the most important job of any of us, she is a full-time, stay-at-home mother. In the past Kelly has worked in the managed care world, taking care of people and their insurance. Currently Kelly spends all her time producing nourishment for little AJ and cleaning up after him and the dogs. In Kelly’s spare time she enjoys doing cross-stitch, shopping and watching movies with Tony.
Becky was only blessed with one brother but she got the cream of the crop when it comes to brothers and sisters-in-law!
 

 
 
Anthony Hren Jr (AJ) Anthony Junior (AJ) is the newest addition to the Hren family. AJ was born on May 16th of this year and has been a little farting ray of sunshine ever since. He does not do much right now except eat, sleep and poop, but he does it cuter than any other baby we know.
 
Flint and Bluto With them in Minnesota they have a fish and the two best dogs in the world, Flint, the prodigy child and Bluto, well, lets just say he is the second child!
